Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Oakville Preplan ("the App") is designed for operational building preplanning and emergency response use. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled within the App.

Information We Collect

The App may collect and store the following types of information:

  • Building information entered by users (addresses, access details, operational notes)
  • Uploaded photos and PDF documents
  • User authentication information (email address)

How Information Is Used

Information collected within the App is used solely for operational planning and emergency response purposes. Data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Data is securely stored using Firebase services provided by Google. Reasonable safeguards are used to protect stored information.

Photo & File Access

The App requests access to the device photo library solely to allow users to upload building-related images. The App does not access photos without user action.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase (Google) for authentication, database storage, and file storage. Firebase may collect technical information necessary to provide these services.

Data Retention

Data remains stored until removed by the user or deleted by the organization managing the App.

Children’s Privacy

This App is intended for professional use and is not directed toward children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
